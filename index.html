About James Baldo


James Baldo hails from Middletown, New Jersey located at the jersey shore. James is currently a junior journalism major at the University of Maryland with a concentration in sports broadcasting.

James has experience working with the Big Ten Network for the University of Maryland, where he has experience working with game cameras and other production roles such as score board operator, replay camera, and game analysis camera. In his spare time, James has reported on COVID vaccinations and PG county budgets for the Prince George's Sentinel. When home at the jersey shore, James helps operate the social media accounts, highlight packages and everyday operations for the Normandy Baskebtall League.
